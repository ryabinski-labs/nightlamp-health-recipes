# Support

## What we offer

This is a community knowledge base maintained by volunteers. We do **not**
provide commercial support, SLAs, or guaranteed response times.

| Topic | Best channel |
|---|---|
| Bug in a recipe (wrong YAML, broken URL, outdated guidance) | [Open an issue](../../issues/new/choose) |
| Idea for a new recipe | [Open a recipe request](../../issues/new?template=recipe-request.yml) |
| Question about a specific recipe | [Start a discussion](../../discussions) if enabled, or open an issue |
| Security report | See [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) |
| Help running Nightlamp the product | https://nightlamp.app/docs |

## What we do not support

- Production incident triage. The recipes are a starting point, not a
  consulting service.
- Custom-written recipes for closed-source vendor configurations.
- One-on-one debugging of customer accounts on other monitoring tools.

## Response expectations

Maintainers review new issues and PRs on a best-effort basis. Recipes are
also reviewed quarterly during a scheduled pass. Stale or unverified recipes
may be archived.
